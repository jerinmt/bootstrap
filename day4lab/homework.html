Frequently Asked Questions

How are the service intervals calculated?
For your BMW, service is not routinely performed at fixed intervals. Instead, it is based on actual vehicle use (number of cold starts, driving habits, type of driving: urban traffic, motorway, etc.). With "Condition Based Services" (CBS), the condition of your BMW is monitored by sensors and special algorithms. For a new vehicle, a theoretical date will be set for the first service, which will be adjusted as your vehicle is used. You will only be notified in the event of a required service.
Is it possible to delete all stored destinations in navigation?
To delete all stored navigation destinations in the navigation system of your BMW with Operating System 7, select: "NAV" > "My destinations" and mark an entry. Press the "Option" button on the right side under the iDrive Controller and confirm "Delete entire list".
What to do if there is a breakdown?
In the event of a breakdown or accident, you can call BMW Roadside Assistance using the My BMW App. Select "Call Roadside Assistance" from the "Vehicle" or "Profile" tab. The location, status and VIN of your vehicle will be transferred to the service employee. Depending on availability in your country, the My BMW App shows you the processing status of your case and the position of the BMW Service Vehicle on its way to you.
