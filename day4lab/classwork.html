Frequently Asked Questions

Where can one get your products?
Our products are available in all department stores large and small across the state of Kerala. We also provide our products in selected stores in Chennai and Bengaluru.
What all products are available?
We produce high quality painting products suited for kids, hobbyists and professional artists.
What to do if there is a faulty product?
Our distribution unit conducts an extensive procedure to ensure there aren't any faults in our products. Still, in the unlikely event of any faults you can contact us at our customer care number to initiate further proceedings.
Can I get any specific colour that is not available in the standard set?
Of course, our Custom Colour Catering allows exactly this. You can send the colour code and we will send it to you. The following link will take you to the particular page: https://colorsofsky/ccc/
